Rooms

Suite Room



The Suite room offers a separate living room complete with a sleeper sofa and a bedroom. This bedroom features a luxurious 200x200 cm sized bed with duck & down mattress topper, luxury 400 thread count silky cotton bed linen, coffee machine, laptop size safe, mini bar and flat LCD TV. All Suites have small balconies with the beautiful views of the Port Dickson Beach and the ocean. Guests are pampered with an evening turndown service. The size of the room is 55 sqm.

Features:
  • Bathroom with double sink and bathtub
  • L'Occitane luxurious bathroom amenities (certified 100% natural ingredients and paraben free)
  • An additional direct telephone in the bathroom
  • Air conditioning
  • Bed size 200x200 cm (with additional 160x200 cm beds)
  • Super luxurious 400 thread count silky cotton bed linen
  • Very soft and very hard down & feather pillows
  • Duck down & feather duvet and mattress topper
  • An extensive pillow menu
  • Flat screen LCD with cable IP TV
  • Video on demand and pay TV channels
  • Direct telephone
  • Nescafe espresso machine
  • Fully stocked mini bar
  • Laptop size electronic safe
  • Bathrobe and cotton slippers
  • Complimentary high speed wireless internet access
  • Evening turndown service with lavander pillow mist
  • Iron with ironing board
  • Suite size: 55 m2

Price: RM220 per night

The room offers a 200x200cm sized bed with duck & down mattress topper, luxury 400 thread count silky cotton bed linen, coffee machine, laptop size safe, mini bar and a flat LCD TV. Guests are pampered with an evening turndown service. The size of the room is 32-48 sqm.

Economy Room



The Economy room, the smallest of all our rooms, is a well-appointed room perfect for a single guest. The bedroom is very comfortable and includes Slon's bed concept with a cosy 140x200 cm mattress, pillow menu, 250 thread count crispy cotton bed linen, safe, kettle with coffee and tea and very well stocked mini bar. The room is air conditioned, and has a flat LCD TV. The bathroom is equipped with shower facilities. The size of the room is 16sqm.

Features:
  • Bathroom with shower
  • Contemporary bath amenities based on natural ingredients and paraben free
  • Air conditioning
  • Bed size 140x200 cm
  • 250 thread count crispy cotton bed linen
  • Very soft and very hard hypoallergenic pillows
  • An extensive pillow menu
  • Flat screen LCD TV, cable IP TV
  • Video on demand and pay TV channels
  • Fully stocked mini bar
  • Laptop size digital safe
  • Direct telephone
